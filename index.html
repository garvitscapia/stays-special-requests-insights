Smoking vs Non-Smoking

11.4% of all guests have a smoking preference (641 smoking + 432 non-smoking = 1,073). 310 requests oddly match BOTH patterns ‚Äî likely saying "non-smoking room." This is the single most automatable preference: a simple toggle at booking would eliminate 1,000+ special requests.
Bed type is the next most automatable: 1,013 requests (king/double + twin) could be a dropdown. Combined with smoking, these two toggles alone would eliminate ~2,000 requests (21%).
5. Growth & Volume Trends

Monthly Request Volume

Explosive growth: From ~30 requests/month in Aug 2023 to 1,009 in Jan 2026 ‚Äî a 33x increase. The growth accelerated significantly from mid-2025 onward, with H2 2025 averaging ~750/month vs H1 2025 at ~230/month. Feb 2026 is on track for another record month (459 already by the 13th).

Attach Rate: Stable at ~12.8%

Despite a 6x growth in total booking volume from Jan 2024 to Dec 2025, the attach rate has remained remarkably flat at ~12.8%. This stability suggests the current free-text input acts as a ceiling ‚Äî only users who already know what to ask for are using it. A structured form could unlock the remaining 87%.
